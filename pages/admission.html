We offer flexible learning options, modern facilities, and a supportive environment that prepares you for global opportunities

Undergraduate Requirements

Applicants seeking admission into our undergraduate programs must provide:

  1. Completed application form
  2. Minimum of five credit passes in relevant subjects at SSCE/WASSCE/NECO or equivalent
  3. A valid means of identification
  4. Recent passport photograph
  5. For some programs, additional screening or interviews may be required

Postgraduate Requirements

For admission into master's programs, applicants are expected to submit:

  1. A recognized bachelor’s degree in a related field
  2. Official academic transcripts
  3. Curriculum Vitae
  4. Two academic or professional reference letters (where applicable)
  5. Statement of purpose outlining career goals
  6. Additional program-specific requirements as stated by the faculty

International Students

International applicants are welcome at Westbridge and must provide the following:

  • A valid passport
  • Equivalent secondary school or bachelor’s degree results
  • English language proficiency (where applicable)
  • Visa and immigration documents as required
  • Proof of financial support or sponsorship

Our international office provides full guidance for travel, immigration, and campus integration
